My life as a dragon Traniner

One Night in my childhood when I was walking by the river. I found a large egg, first I thought it might be a stone because it was weird and had different colours and textures so I took it to my garage. I Called my friend to show this stone to him and then he said it was an egg. From that day I started taking care of the egg. I used to keep it in a warm place and also the fact hiding it from my parents.

After weeks, one day it started cracking and there was a dinosaur baby inside it. I was astonished since I always had fantasy about dragons. Then I started babysitting the dinosaur and used to feed it milk (just like anyone would do). So i learned "how to train your Dragon" from the internet and i trained it, how to walk, how to open wings, how to fly. It got bigger in a few weeks. Now it was difficult to hid it from my parents. So I told them and they refused to accept the dinosaur.I fought with them but was of no use.

With so much disappointment, I took it to the forest and leave it there. But on the next day, I was very nervous about the dinosaur. I went back to the forest(with some food) to the exact point where I left it. It wasn't there and I felt very bad thinking it might have left the town. After a few weeks i again went to the same place. It wasn't there still, but while leaving the forest i heard Noises which dinosaurs used to make when it was still with me and suddenly I looked up it was there flying above the forest and also it was able to throw fire. I was very happy to see it again. I was proud that my training did work.

Now i used to daily visit the dragon after my School and feed it. Also, the dragon took me to fly on its back all over the town.
